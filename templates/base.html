Greetings, dear sir/madam/miss, and welcome. This is a demonstration page for the project BibCrunch. Here you can see a table for degrees of book similarity (1=exact copy, 0=completely different). You can find these books at Project Gutenberg by their id. The books have been selected at random from all the English books available. For example, the number at "id_a" and "0" is 65140. To find this book, paste the number into the quick search bar at the Project Gutenberg website and hit enter.

Under the table (you'll have to scroll) there's a little box. You can send text files through the box and the system will tell you how similar the two text files are dynamically. Tip: if you have a personal computer and you're using a keyboard, you can usually press the end key to quickly scroll to the bottom.
